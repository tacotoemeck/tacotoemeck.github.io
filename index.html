About me



Ahoy, I’m Tomasz but everyone calls me Tom. I live in London and I am currently in training to become a Front-End Developer.


Although I started developing websites aged 13, it wasn’t until summer ’19 when I really decided to make it into a living. Since I took a very long break, many things have changed, and I had a lot of catching up to do. Coding is my passion so it’s not difficult spending literally every spare moment re-learning it.


Beforehand I had a good run in sales and then recruitment followed by making a dream of opening a taco shop true. Last one is now run by my wife and still makes best of London’s tacos.


On a personal level. I love backpacking, nature, hikes, climbing, food, my cat and most importantly my beautiful wife Monica whose support allows me dedicate myself to making my passion into a career.


Feel free to visit my learning journal on my github profile here: tacotomek or connect with me on CodeWars if you wanted to pair programme some challenges.


I am interested in finding a junior/trainee role where I will be able to work with JavaScript. I have chosen to get a solid understanding of CSS3, JavaScript, Algorithms, Data Structures and basic CS before learning libraries so I’m still an open book. I look forward to learning anything new so get in touch if you’re looking for someone passionate, dedicated and willing to learn
